---

## Overview

The **Revenue Analytics Dashboard** provides a focused view of revenue generation across a simulated global streaming platform inspired by Netflix. Built using a synthetic dataset designed to mirror real-world streaming analytics systems, this dashboard enables analysts and decision-makers to understand how revenue is distributed across subscription plans and geographic markets.

The dashboard highlights **key financial indicators, revenue distribution across subscription tiers, and regional revenue patterns**, enabling a quick evaluation of how different markets and pricing plans contribute to the platform’s overall revenue performance.

Using KPI cards, interactive slicers, donut charts, and large comparison visuals, the dashboard allows users to explore revenue insights across geographic regions and subscription plans.

This dashboard answers key analytical questions such as:

- How much total revenue is generated by the platform?
- How many active subscriptions contribute to revenue?
- What is the average revenue generated per user (ARPU)?
- Which countries and regions generate the most revenue?
- Which subscription plans contribute the largest share of revenue?

---

## Business Storyline & Analytical Flow

### 1. Revenue KPI Snapshot – KPI Cards

Five KPI cards provide a quick summary of the platform’s revenue performance:

- **Total Revenue**  
- **Total Active Subscriptions**  
- **Average Revenue per User (ARPU)**  
- **Total Subscribers**  
- **Revenue per Subscription Plan**

These KPIs provide an immediate overview of revenue scale and subscription activity.

---

### 2. Revenue Distribution Visualization

The dashboard includes a **donut chart** that shows how revenue is distributed across different subscription plans:

- **REVENUE BY SUBSCRIPTION PLAN** – Donut Chart

This visual allows stakeholders to quickly evaluate which pricing tiers contribute the most to platform revenue.

---

### 3. Regional Revenue Insights

Three large visuals provide deeper insights into revenue distribution across geographic levels:

1. **REVENUE BY COUNTRY** – Bar Chart  
2. **REVENUE BY REGION** – Clustered Column Chart  
3. **REVENUE BY SUBSCRIPTION PLAN** – Clustered Bar Chart  

These visuals allow analysts to identify high-performing markets and understand how subscription plans perform across different geographic regions.

---

### 4. Interactive Slicers

Six slicers enable dynamic filtering of the dashboard:

- Continent (`Dim_Geography[Continent]`)
- Country (`Dim_Geography[Country]`)
- Subscription Plan (`Dim_SubscriptionPlan[PlanName]`)
- Year (`Dim_Time[Year]`)
- Quarter (`Dim_Time[Quarter]`)
- Month (`Dim_Time[Month]`)

These filters allow users to explore revenue patterns across multiple geographic and subscription dimensions.

---

## Key Takeaways

This dashboard helps stakeholders:

- Monitor overall revenue performance of the platform
- Identify which subscription plans generate the most revenue
- Compare revenue contribution across countries and regions
- Understand subscription-driven revenue distribution
- Quickly filter revenue insights by geography and plan type
