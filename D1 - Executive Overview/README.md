## Overview

The **Executive Overview Dashboard** is the **top-most dashboard** of the Global Streaming Intelligence Suite, designed specifically for **decision-makers and leadership teams**.

It provides a **high-level, consolidated view of platform performance**, integrating insights across **users, revenue, engagement, content, and churn**.

This dashboard acts as a **central command layer**, enabling executives to monitor overall business health while serving as a **navigation entry point to the remaining 14 detailed dashboards**.

---

## Strategic Purpose

This dashboard answers key executive questions:

* What is the current scale of the platform?  
* How are revenue and engagement performing globally?  
* Which content and genres drive the most value?  
* How is the user base distributed across regions?  
* What are the key churn and retention patterns?  

It enables **fast, data-driven decision-making without deep technical exploration**.

---

## Dashboard Structure

### 1. Executive KPI Layer

Eight KPI cards provide a snapshot of overall performance:

* Total Users  
* Total Revenue  
* Total Watch Hours  
* Total Content  
* Avg Rating  
* Total Streams  
* Churn Rate (%)  
* ARPU  

These KPIs summarize **growth, engagement, monetization, and retention** in a single view.

---

### 2. Main Visual Insights

Seven visuals provide strategic insights across key business dimensions:

1. **Global Performance Map** – Global engagement by watch hours  
2. **Revenue by Plan** – Revenue contribution by subscription tier  
3. **Watch Hours by Genre** – Content consumption patterns  
4. **User Distribution by Region** – Regional user base split  
5. **Top 10 Content by Watch Hours** – High-performing titles  
6. **Churn vs Active Users** – Retention analysis across plans  
7. **Content Contribution Treemap** – Genre and title-level impact  

These visuals ensure executives can quickly identify **growth drivers, risks, and opportunities**.

---

### 3. Interactive Filters

Eight slicers enable dynamic exploration:

* Continent  
* Country  
* Subscription Plan  
* Content Type  
* Genre  
* Language  
* Gender  
* Region  

These allow stakeholders to slice insights across **geography, content, and user segments**.
